Welcome to Baiyun Mountain

Let's enjoy the beauty of nature

Baiyun Mountain, known as "the most beautiful mountain in Guangzhou", is located in the northeast of Guangzhou City. It covers a total area of 21.8 square kilometers and is a national grade-AAAAA scenic area and the only national scenic area in Guangzhou.

As the ‘city lung’ of Guangzhou, Baiyun Mountain is not only a treasure trove of urban ecological resources, but also a gene pool of plants and animals. According to the latest survey data, the green coverage of Baiyun Mountain has reached an astonishing 95%, with an average negative ion content of 3,000-7,000/cm³, reaching the national level standard. It is not only a good place for public leisure, but also a paradise for wildlife.
